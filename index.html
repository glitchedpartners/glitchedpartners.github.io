Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about solar energy and our services

Savings depend on your current electricity bill and system size. Most customers save 60-90% on their electricity bills. A typical 10kW system can save PKR 25,000-35,000 monthly, with payback periods of 3-5 years.

Net metering allows you to sell excess solar energy back to the grid. When your panels produce more electricity than you use, the surplus is fed into the grid, and you receive credits on your electricity bill. We handle all net metering paperwork and approvals.

Physical installation typically takes 1-3 days depending on system size. The complete process from consultation to grid connection usually takes 2-4 weeks, including permits and utility approvals.

We provide comprehensive coverage: 25-year panel performance warranty, 10-year inverter warranty, 5-year system warranty, and 2-year installation warranty. All warranties are backed by manufacturer guarantees.

Yes, solar panels work in cloudy conditions, though at reduced efficiency. Pakistan's climate provides excellent solar conditions year-round. Our systems are designed to maximize energy production even in varying weather conditions.

Solar panels require minimal maintenance. Regular cleaning (quarterly) and annual inspections are recommended. Our monitoring system alerts you to any issues, and we provide maintenance services as needed.
